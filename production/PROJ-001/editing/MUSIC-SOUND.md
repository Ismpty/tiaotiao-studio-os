# 1. Purpose（目的）

## 中文

本文件用于指导《跳跳下班啦》试播项目的音乐、音效、环境声和混音方向。

声音系统的目标不是制造强刺激，而是帮助观众感受到：

- 工作结束后的松弛
- 下班后的自由感
- 生活重新开始的温暖
- Jump / 跳跳 的日常陪伴感

音乐和音效必须服务故事，不能压过画面，也不能把短片剪成广告、综艺或焦虑内容。

## English

This file defines the music, sound design, ambience and audio mix direction for PROJ-001 — Jump After Work Pilot Project.

The sound should support the warm after-work emotion without overpowering the story.

# 4. Music Direction（音乐方向）

## Recommended Music Style

```text
Warm lo-fi
Light acoustic
Soft indie pop instrumental
Gentle piano texture
Light guitar
Warm lifestyle beat
Soft evening ambience
```

## Music Feeling

```text
温暖
轻松
不压迫
有生活感
有一点自由感
不苦情
不焦虑
不商业广告感
```

## Avoid

```text
强鼓点卡点
高压电子乐
赛博朋克电子
恐怖氛围音乐
悲伤苦情音乐
广告大片感音乐
综艺感音乐
过度甜腻可爱音乐
过度燃向音乐
```

# 5. Recommended Music Structure（推荐音乐结构）

## 45s Timeline

```text
0:00–0:06
Soft intro, very light rhythm.

0:06–0:12
Warm melody enters when laptop closes.

0:12–0:19
Light rhythm continues while Jump packs.

0:19–0:29
Music opens slightly as Jump walks out.

0:29–0:37
Warm lift during door transition.

0:37–0:45
Soft emotional hold, gentle ending.
```

# 6. Shot-by-Shot Sound Direction（逐镜头声音方向）

## Shot 1 — Work Ending

```text
Music:
Very soft intro, minimal rhythm.

Sound Effects:
Soft keyboard typing.
Subtle room tone.
Laptop fan or quiet office ambience.

Feeling:
Workday ending, calm relief.

Avoid:
Do not use loud typing.
Do not make the office feel stressful.
```

---

## Shot 2 — Decision Moment

```text
Music:
Warm melody begins or becomes slightly clearer.

Sound Effects:
Laptop closing sound.
Soft chair movement.
Very light room ambience.

Feeling:
A small emotional decision.

Avoid:
Do not use dramatic sound hit.
Do not make laptop closing sound too sharp.
```

---

## Shot 3 — Packing Up

```text
Music:
Light rhythm continues.

Sound Effects:
Backpack zipper.
Notebook movement.
Headphones or small object sound.
Soft fabric movement.

Feeling:
Daily routine, tactile transition.

Avoid:
Do not make object sounds too loud.
Do not over-design every object.
```

---

## Shot 4 — Walking Out

```text
Music:
Music opens slightly, more movement, but still gentle.

Sound Effects:
Soft footsteps.
Backpack fabric movement.
Subtle tail / body movement sound only if natural.

Feeling:
Freedom begins.

Avoid:
Do not use strong beat drop.
Do not make footsteps heavy or dramatic.
```

---

## Shot 5 — Door Exit

```text
Music:
Warm lift, slightly brighter emotion.

Sound Effects:
Door handle.
Door opening.
Soft outside ambience entering.

Feeling:
Transition from work to after-work life.

Avoid:
No dramatic escape sound.
No loud door slam.
No harsh wind.
```

---

## Shot 6 — Warm Ending

```text
Music:
Gentle emotional hold.
Soft ending tail.

Sound Effects:
Light evening ambience.
Soft city or room tone.
Optional small breath / quiet pause feeling.

Feeling:
Life begins again after work.

Avoid:
Do not end abruptly.
Do not use commercial logo sting.
Do not overdo emotional music.
```

# 7. Ambient Sound Direction（环境声方向）

环境声应该很轻，只增加真实感。

推荐环境声：

```text
soft room tone
distant city ambience
light evening ambience
subtle office air
very soft footsteps
quiet fabric movement
```

避免环境声：

```text
crowded office noise
traffic noise too loud
alarm sound
phone notification sound
keyboard noise too intense
machine hum too strong
```

## Voice Direction

```text
温柔
自然
轻声
像生活记录

不鸡汤

不营销
```

```text

65–75%

20–30%

5–10%
```

# 12. Audio Level Principles（音量原则）

```text
[ ] 音乐不能压过旁白
[ ] 音效不能抢戏
[ ] 开头音量不要突然冲出来
[ ] 结尾不要突然断掉
[ ] 每个镜头之间声音过渡自然
[ ] Shot 6 结尾要有轻微收束
```

---

# 13. Transition Sound Rules（转场声音规则）

推荐：

```text
natural sound bridge
soft ambience continuation
cut on object sound
light music phrase transition
```

避免：

```text
whoosh
swipe
flash sound
boom
glitch
template transition sound
overly cute pop
```

本片转场应该自然，不需要明显提示“这里转场了”。

# 14. Platform Sound Notes（平台声音备注）

## 小红书

```text
声音要温暖、轻松、有生活方式感。
可以保留音乐和轻字幕，不一定需要旁白。
```

## 抖音

```text
前 3 秒声音要清楚，但不要刺激。
可以让电脑关闭声或音乐进入点帮助观众理解转折。
```

## 视频号

```text
声音要自然，不要太网感。
更适合轻音乐 + 温柔字幕。
```

## YouTube Shorts / TikTok / Reels

```text
国际平台可以使用无旁白版本。
音乐和画面情绪要更直观。
```
