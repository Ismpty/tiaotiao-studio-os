# 3. Fixed Storyboard Visual Style（固定故事板视觉风格）

故事板主体必须采用：

```text
black-and-white line art
rough pencil lines
minimal details
fast dynamic sketching
simple anatomy
clear silhouettes
lightweight unfinished storyboard feeling
director previs sketch feeling
```

禁止：

```text
full-color concept art
polished illustration
realistic final render
children's picture book style
fully colored comic style
```

---

# 4. Storyboard Color Annotation System（故事板彩色标注系统）

```text
Red = camera movement
Blue = character / dog movement
Yellow = prop interaction
Green = lighting / mood
Purple = continuity / transition
```

彩色标注只用于箭头、圈注和说明，不改变故事板主体的黑白铅笔线稿风格。

# 7. Shot Details（逐镜头分镜）

## Shot 1 — Work Ending

```text
Storyboard Panel Description:
Black-and-white rough pencil panel. Jump is a fluffy real dog-form character wearing programmer-style dog clothes, sitting at a warm creator desk. The laptop screen and desk lamp create soft light on her face. Her small dog paws relax near the keyboard.

Camera:
Medium desk shot, 35mm natural perspective, eye-level framing.

Motion:
Small relaxed paw movement, shoulders softening, workday ending.

Lighting / Mood:
Warm screen glow and desk lamp glow. Calm relief.

Color Annotation:
Red: small static camera frame mark.
Blue: tiny motion marks showing paws relaxing.
Yellow: circle laptop and keyboard.
Green: mark screen glow and desk lamp.
Purple: note laptop remains open for next shot.

Forbidden Drift:
No human hands, no human arms, no humanoid body, no cold corporate office, no polished colored illustration.
```

## Shot 2 — Decision Moment

```text
Storyboard Panel Description:
Black-and-white rough pencil close-up. Jump looks toward the window. Golden-hour light enters the studio. She gently closes the laptop with a small dog-form movement. Her expression shifts from focused to quietly hopeful.

Camera:
Medium close-up, gentle push-in, eye-level.

Motion:
Head turn toward window, laptop closing, soft smile.

Lighting / Mood:
Screen glow fades, window light becomes stronger. Expectation and freedom.

Color Annotation:
Red: push-in arrow.
Blue: head-turn arrow.
Yellow: circle laptop hinge.
Green: window light direction.
Purple: note laptop closed for continuity.

Forbidden Drift:
No exaggerated acting, no human hand close-up, no dramatic commercial pose.
```

## Shot 3 — Packing Up

```text
Storyboard Panel Description:
Black-and-white rough pencil detail panels. Jump packs a notebook, headphones and small items into her small backpack. The action feels tactile and everyday, not staged.

Camera:
Detail close-ups, 50mm feeling, shallow but sketchy composition.

Motion:
Small dog-form prop interaction, backpack zipper closing, items moving into bag.

Lighting / Mood:
Warm practical desk lamp and soft ambient fill. Gentle transition.

Color Annotation:
Red: small panel-to-panel camera marks.
Blue: movement arrows for Jump's body and head.
Yellow: circles around notebook, headphones and backpack zipper.
Green: desk lamp glow.
Purple: note backpack becomes part of Jump's state.

Forbidden Drift:
No human hand anatomy, no chaotic desk, no over-staged product shot.
```

## Shot 4 — Walking Out

```text
Storyboard Panel Description:
Black-and-white rough pencil wide vertical panel. Jump walks through the studio wearing her small backpack. Her body remains four-legged dog-form, with natural grounded movement and subtle tail motion.

Camera:
Hero tracking camera, 35mm eye-level follow, documentary feel.

Motion:
Natural dog walking, realistic weight shift, grounded paw contact.

Lighting / Mood:
Warm studio depth. Freedom begins.

Color Annotation:
Red: camera tracking arrow following Jump.
Blue: dog movement path across the room.
Yellow: small backpack state marker.
Green: warm studio light zones.
Purple: direction toward door connects to next shot.

Forbidden Drift:
No bipedal human walking, no sliding feet, no robotic motion, no game-camera framing.
```

## Shot 5 — Door Exit

```text
Storyboard Panel Description:
Black-and-white rough pencil rear-follow panel. Jump reaches the office door. The door opens toward brighter after-work light. The frame feels open and hopeful.

Camera:
Rear follow shot, natural 35mm movement.

Motion:
Dog-form walking, small pause, door opening.

Lighting / Mood:
Warm interior light transitions into golden outside light. Release and openness.

Color Annotation:
Red: rear-follow camera arrow.
Blue: Jump's path through the doorway.
Yellow: circle door handle / doorway interaction.
Green: indoor-to-outdoor light gradient.
Purple: transition note into after-work world.

```

# 8. Storyboard Prompt（模型可读故事板提示词）

```text
Create a 6-shot vertical storyboard for a warm short video called Jump After Work.

The storyboard body must be black-and-white rough pencil line art with minimal details, fast dynamic sketching, simple anatomy, clear silhouettes and an unfinished director previs sketch feeling. Do not make polished colored concept art, realistic final render, children's picture book art or fully colored comic panels.

Jump is a fluffy real dog-form character wearing programmer-style dog clothes. Jump has four-legged dog anatomy, dog proportions, fluffy fur, warm friendly expression, a small backpack, optional badge or collar charm, and subtle dragon-fruit pink accent details. Do not draw Jump as a humanoid body, human hands, human arms, human legs, bipedal human standing pose, another animal or an unclothed ordinary pet dog.

Use colored annotations only for production notes:
red arrows for camera movement, blue arrows for Jump's dog movement, yellow circles for prop interaction, green marks for lighting and mood, purple notes for continuity and transition.

The story should show Jump finishing work, deciding to leave, packing up, walking out, exiting the door and ending in warm after-work light.
```
